About Chi Ki Prana

Chi Ki Prana is Elizabeth Borge’s wellness practice, created as a place for healing, movement, and personal balance.

About Elizabeth Borge

Chi Ki Prana is an expression of Elizabeth Borge’s lifelong connection with movement, wellness, and healing. Her background brings together yoga therapy, Reiki, Tai Chi, nutrition, personal training, and dance.

Elizabeth’s path has included ballet, jazz, modern, tap, and hip hop, along with a deeper study of the body, breath, energy, and practical wellness habits. This gives her work a grounded, movement-informed foundation.

Through Chi Ki Prana, Elizabeth supports clients who want to feel stronger, calmer, more balanced, and more connected to themselves. Sessions are personal, thoughtful, and shaped around the person in front of her.

Her Philosophy

Healing is not one single method. It is movement, rest, nutrition, awareness, energy, and support working together over time.
