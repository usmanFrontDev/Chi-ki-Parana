FAQ

Everything You Need to Know Before You Book

Have a question we haven't answered? Reach out via the form below.

What is the minimum rental time?

Our minimum rental is 1 hour. We also offer half-day (4-hour) and full-day (8-hour) blocks. Custom durations may be accommodated — just ask.

Can I visit the space before booking?

Absolutely. We encourage you to see the space first. Reach out through our inquiry form and we'll arrange a short walkthrough at a mutually convenient time.

What equipment is provided?

Both rooms include yoga mats, props (blocks, straps, bolsters), a Bluetooth sound system, and Wi-Fi. The Healing Room also includes a Reiki/massage table. A full equipment list is available on request.

Can I bring my own clients and students?

Yes! The space is yours during your booked time. You can bring clients, students, or workshop participants. We just ask that you manage the group within the capacity limits of the room.

Is parking available for my clients?

Yes, free on-site parking is available for both practitioners and their clients.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refund with 48 hours notice. 50% refund with 24 hours notice. No refund for same-day cancellations.

Can I book a recurring time slot?

Yes — our Premium (Full Day) package includes the option for a recurring weekly slot. Regular practitioners on other packages may also request a preferred recurring time, subject to availability.

Is the space suitable for online or hybrid sessions?

Yes. Fast Wi-Fi and Bluetooth audio are available in both rooms. Many of our practitioners run virtual or hybrid sessions with no issues.
